You're now reading: why i like animals!

the 2008 album by TTNG



So yeah...

  • it makes me think good
    • post2000pre2010 mathrock shenanigans are everywhere in this album
    • twinkly riffs
    • bpm go nyoom mostly
    • real unconventional song structure
    • the unpredictability of sections just st-
    • can't forget to mention time changes
    • -opping and starting again
    • rich harmony that isn't overbearing
  • it makes me feel good
    • Even with all the chaos there's wonderful lyrics and melodies to hang on to
    • I've never truly been emo i've never had my MCR phase and yet... there's something about the words Stu's written that always gets me.
    • It keeps me grounded in trying to receive and give music as a person too instead of just someone who only knows music theory
    • I've gotten to know this album a lot better at an important time in my life

and with the 2018 acoustic release, it just feels like so comforting, it's as if i've taken some time to grow without the album and when i met it again in a new light it felt like a warm hug
