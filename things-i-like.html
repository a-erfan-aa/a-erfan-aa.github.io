A few things before you go on reading...

  • I just like these things okay i'm no expert on any of em
  • These are all personal opinions, i'm not saying anything here is objectively good
  • I try to list things in here regardless of their fanbases, no matter how cringe they get
  • I try to list things in here regardless of whoever made them
    • Because of that, you might see names like C. Dodgson and R. Wagner because i think their works are very entertaining or important, in spite of the questionable stuff surrounding them.
    • That being said, there should be names like Gus & Eddy that i put in here because they make good stuff AND they seem like swell folk too even tho i've never known them personally

Outer Wilds
  • Singleplayer space puzzle exploration game by Mobius Digital, and not the Outer Worlds by other company
  • Go into it blind if you want to try it! only look at a trailer and spoiler free reviews if you must!
  • I've had no problems at all and found it an absolutely incredible experience, there's so much charm in the whole solar system
  • Great soundtrack by Andrew Prahlow too (youtube)
  • Some valid criticisms i've seen however:
    • 6 degrees of freedom might make for big motion sickness, and i've seen a couple claims of bad controls
    • Some players find the timing/pace of how puzzles/story progress not good
    • Some weird stuff about Mobius Digital initially releasing exclusively on epic games, backers who kickstarted for xbox/steam releases had to wait longer than promised
