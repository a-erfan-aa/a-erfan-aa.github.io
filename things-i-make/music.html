A foreword of sorts

What i have here is currently more of my plans and progress rather than a catalogue of my works, though i hope that'll change within a year.

I've spent most of my time scratching and fleshing out ideas in musescore, so i don't really have any demos available, nor do i have any plans to release stuff anytime soon. But someday i think i will i'm quite sure.

A musical work for me would ideally follow the following life cycle:

  • Conceptualizing
  • Writing
  • Recording
  • Mixing
  • Mastering
  • Release
