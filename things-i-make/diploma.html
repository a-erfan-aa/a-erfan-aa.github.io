What's goin on

My seniors before me finished off their diploma in music performance by holding an exit recital of sorts as their final project. I can't do that because of what i hope are obvious reasons so i changed the scope of the project to a live recording of my setlist instead.

It's not as ideal as other options i have but i've cleared it with the people involved, so i'm gonna pull this off even if it takes me 5 years at the rate government issued STAY INDOORS notices keep being handed out.

I also plan to edit a final product for submissions and a different final product to shove onto my youtube channel because why the heck not. It's a fun enough setlist to show people but i need to maintain some air of professionalism to get my grades so i thought "why not just record once cut twice?".
