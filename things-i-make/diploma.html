What's goin on

My seniors before me finished off their diploma in music performance by holding an exit recital of sorts as their final project. I don't feel comfortable doing that because of what i hope are obvious reasons so i changed the scope of the project to a live-ish recording of my setlist instead.

I plan to edit a final product for submissions and a different final product to shove onto my youtube channel because why the heck not. It's a fun enough setlist to show a general audience but i need to maintain some air of professionalism to get my grades so i thought "why not just record once and cut twice?".

I've had to rework the project multiple times since early 2020 due to a buncha reasons but i hope this is the one that sticks and i can finally get it done.
