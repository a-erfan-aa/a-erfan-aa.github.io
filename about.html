My other names and tags

My name's abang erfan, pronounced ah·bahng air·fahn, you can call me erfan for short. There are a couple people who call me abang but that's pretty much a... family name? Afaik there isn't a clear analogue between malay malaysian names to the western firstnamelastname format of names. You can also call me kurt since that's the screenname i roll with, and definitely nothing else.

The whole website is pretty much an aboutme so i'd encourage you to go through the other pages if you wanna know more about me, i don't do well when put on the spot to talk about myself.
